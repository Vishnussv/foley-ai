# Mobile Installation Guide

Since FOLEY controls your PC, the "App" is actually a connection to your computer. Follow these steps to install the FOLEY interface on your phone.

## Prerequisites
1. Ensure your PC and Phone are on the **same Wi-Fi network**.
2. Run `run_foleydraco.bat` on your PC.

You now have a "Foleydraco" app icon on your home screen. When you open it:
- It launches in **full screen** (no browser bars).
- It behaves exactly like a native app.
- It connects directly to your PC's neuro-core.
